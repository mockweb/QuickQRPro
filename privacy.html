Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Today

At QuickQR Pro, your privacy is our top priority. Because our tool functions completely on the client-side (within your browser), we ensure maximum privacy.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into the QR code generator. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Camera Permissions

The QR Scanner requires access to your device's camera. This access is strictly used in real-time to decode QR codes locally on your device. Video feeds are never recorded or sent to any external server.

3. Analytics & Ads

We use third-party ad networks like Google AdSense to keep this tool free. These networks may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browser settings. We do not control these third-party tracking technologies.
